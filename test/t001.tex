\section{SECTIONS}
Section header starts at column 1 and it must be upper case.
Subsection are not supported and not left-aligned (upper case) text will not
be interpreted as a header.

\section{MATH}
Inline math such as $j=j_0\left(\exp(X)-\exp(-X)\right)$ i supported 
or simple equation environment such as 
$$ j=j_0\left(\exp(X)-\exp(-X)\right) $$
is also supported.

\section{LISTS}
Three list-like environments are supported: \textit{itemize}, \textit{enumerate} and \textit{\textbf{description}}.

The \textit{\textbf{description}} environment is triggered when the line starts with [-*o] followed by a space.
\begin{description}
\item[item] d
\item[item] d
\end{description}

The \textit{itemize} environment is triggered when the line starts with [-*o] followed by a space.
\begin{itemize}
\item item
\item item
\item item
\item item
\end{itemize}

A second list:
\begin{itemize}
\item item with spaces
\end{itemize}
